Carrot Cake Recipe

Here's a link to the AllRecipes page: All Recipes

Prep Time: 30 Minutes
Cook Time: 40 Minutes
Additional Time: 10 Minutes
Total Time: 1 Hour, 20 Minutes
Servings: 18


Ingredients:



  • White Sugar
  • Confectioners' Sugar
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Four Eggs
  • Two tsp. Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda and/or Baking Powder
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Grated Carrots
  • Chopped Pecans
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese

Steps:


  1. Make the batter: Beat wet ingredients together, then mix in dry ingredients. Stir in carrots, and fold in pecans
  2. Bake the cake: Pour batter into prepared cake pan. Bake in preheated oven until toothpick comes out clean
  3. Frost the cake: Beat frosting ingredients together until smooth and creamy. Stir in pecans. Frost the cooled cake
