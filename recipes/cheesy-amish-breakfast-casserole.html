Cheesy Amish Breakfast Casserole Recipe

Here's a link to the All Recipes page:

Prep Time: 10 Minutes
Cook Time: 55 Minutes
Additional Time: 10 Minutes
Total Time: 1 Hour, 15 Minutes
Servings: 12


Ingredients:



  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • 9 Eggs
  • Hash Browns
  • Cheese (Cheddar, Swiss, and Cottage)

Steps:


  1. Cook bacon and onion together, drain, and transfer to bowl
  2. Stir in eggs, potatoes, and cheeses
  3. Pour into prepared baking dish and bake until eggs are set
