Deviled Eggs Recipe

Here's a link to the AllRecipes page: All Recipes

Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Total Time: 15 Minutes
Servings: 6


Ingredients:



  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Vegetables (onion and celery)
  • Seasonings (salt and paprika)

Steps:


  1. Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half and remove yolks. Reserve the whites
  2. Mash yolks with fork and add remaining ingredients (besides paprika)
  3. Stuff or pipe filling into egg white halves
  4. Top with paprika, and extra salt if needed
